# 2. GENERAL

This erection manual covers the field installation of the two Heat Recovery Steam Generator (HRSG) for the Sun Ba Phase II CCPP project in Taiwan.
The Heat Recovery Steam Generator is located downstream a Siemens SGEN6-9000HL natural gas fired turbine and consists of a natural circulation, triple pressure with reheat unit, of which the high pressure section is a once through Benson system.
The HRSG shall be constructed in accordance with ASME 1, ASME B31.1.
Construction per these standards shall be defined to include, but not limited to, material selection, design, fabrication, examination, inspection testing, certification and pressure relief.
Boilers are to be ASME stamped.
This manual describes the mechanical site installation of the HRSG, covering non-pressure and pressure parts.
The document describes the lay-out and set-up of the HRSG and provides description and guidance on how the HRSG components can be installed at the construction site. The reader must note that the document cannot be used for commercial purposes or scope description.
Alternative erection and/or sequences are possible; however, the erection contractor shall consult with, and obtain written approval from the manufacturer prior to commencing with the chosen alternative installation and/or sequence.
This erection manual shall in no way relieve the erection contractor from its responsibility to comply with local law and requirements, as well as construction code and project specific requirements.

# 4. HRSG DESCRIPTION

Flue gas entrance for the HRSG scope is at the inlet duct. Gas turbine flue gasses enter the HRSG transition-duct and inlet-duct in horizontal direction. The boiler-duct contains the vertically positioned harps with inlet and outlet headers and interconnecting piping. The HRSG is divided into six boxes with an A.I.G. / SCR section in between box two and three. Each box is divided in three gas paths: left harp module, center harp module and a right harp module. Flue gasses exit the boiler through the outlet-duct and stack.
From hot to cold side of the HRSG, the location of the bundle harps are as follows:
- Box 1 - Harps HP / RH Super heater
- Box 2 - Harps HP Evaporator / Separator vessel
- A.I.G. / SCR box
- Box 3 - Harps HP Economizer / IP Super heater
- Box 4 - Harps HP Economizer / IRAC Econ / IP Evaporator
- Box 5 - Harps LP Super heater / IP Economizer / HP Economizer / LP Evaporator
- Box 6 - Harps Condensate Preheater Economizer
The HRSG consists of the following main components;

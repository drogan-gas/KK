
ERECTION SEQUENCE DRAWINGS HRSG COMPONENTS
# General notes 
Note:
This document, including all documents attached and referenced herein, describes the erection sequence of the hrsg for the sunba II project (60031).
Some of the described installation steps can be performed simultaneous, depending on available manpower and equipment. Siemens htt tfa can advise at site regarding any details or questions regarding the erection sequence. E.g. Start of inlet duct and stack erection can start after finalization module erection. Piping erection is performed simultaneous with platform erection.
A timely erection of the staircase is beneficial to allow easy access to hrsg during its erection, contractor is advised to check erection steps of stair tower with regard to hrsg erection, in order to avoid additional works or delays as much as possible.
For both casing/pressure parts as well as for example for the inlet duct and outlet duct/stack, the respective steps for each component are largely grouped, in order to provide an intelligeble overview.
Parallel activities however are possible, depending on available manpower, equipment and working area. Tfa can be consulted for advise on the execution of parallel activities.
Advised is to start with the inlet duct installation in an early stage, parallel to the finishing steps of the
Casing/pressure part sequence. Reason for this is that an early erection of the inlet duct allows for important onward steps, like piping and platform installation above the inlet duct, as well as the main piping header and flow baffle installation between inlet duct and box 1.
The best moment for the start of inlet duct erection activities will have to be checked based on the actual situation at site, between the contractor and tfa. A logical moment would be to start, when casing is a complete (structural) unit.
Adequate paint touch up and field painting including proper surface preparation has to be performed
During the complete erection phase. Touch up and field painting is not in scope of siemens-energy-htt
The requirements of the field quality plans (fqp’s)have to be followed during the complete erection phase

 ## Sequence of harp module arrival – lifting activities at site
 Harp module availability for sunba 2 project is dependent on the delivery from factory. Latest available information at time of generating these sequence drawings, is that the modules for box 6 are first to arrive at site.
 The modules for the remaining boxes will arrive in sequence starting from hrsg stack side towards hrsg inlet duct side:
 - box 5 => box 4 => box 3 => box 2 and lastly box 1
 In order not to delay site works, adviced is to perform installation works of the harp modules in sequence with the availability (arrivals from shop).
 Follow the installation steps 26 u/i 33 for each of the harp modules.
 The proposed installation sequence within each box, is to install the outer modules first (left and right, casing side), followed by the middle module.
 Note:
 In case module arrival differs from above described sequence, please consult htt tfa for possibilities in modifying the lifting sequence accordingly.
